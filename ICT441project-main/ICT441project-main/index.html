Please understand us

As of March 2023, the university has two campuses, the main campus and Meishan Campus,
covering a total area of 3,045 mu, with 3.156 million copies of paper books and 2.434 million copies of electronic books,
and the total value of equipment and equipment is 1.31 billion yuan. It has 23 colleges, 10 university-level research institutes and 3 affiliated hospitals,
offering 59 undergraduate majors. There are 9 first-level doctoral degree programs, 3 post-doctoral research stations,
31 first-level master's degree programs, and 25 professional master's degree authorization categories.

The school's disciplinary construction has achieved significant results. We have 9 national "Double First Class"
construction disciplines, 10 national key disciplines (including 1 cultivation discipline), 59advantageous characteristic
disciplines (clusters) in Zhejiang Province, and 22 first-class key disciplines in Zhejiang province. There are currently 67 various scientific
research institutions, laboratories, and research institutes (centers).
